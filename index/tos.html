2. Payments, Deposits, and Pricing

All pricing is agreed upon before work begins and may vary based on the project's complexity, size, and style.

  • Payment Method: Payments are processed through PayPal unless otherwise agreed upon.

Upon final payment, the Client receives a permanent, worldwide license to use the final build for its agreed-upon purpose (personal use, server use, content creation).

  • The Creator retains full copyright over the original artistic design.
  • The Creator retains the right to showcase the build in their public portfolio and promotional materials.
  • Reselling: Custom commissioned builds are exclusive to the Client and will not be resold by the Creator to other clients, unless explicitly agreed upon in the initial contract.
  • Credit: Clients are only required to provide proper credit to Oalz Creations when using free builds posted and published by the Creator on social media. Credit is not mandatory for commissioned work, but is always appreciated.

4. Project Timeline & Revisions

A clear vision must be provided by the Client before work begins, including size, style, and required features.

  • Timeline: An estimated completion time will be provided before work begins. Any delays due to unforeseen circumstances will be communicated immediately.

  • Troubleshooting: Troubleshooting WorldEdit or Litematica errors is not included in the base price but can be provided at an additional fee of $10 per hour.

6. Refund Policy

The Creator maintains discretion regarding the refund amount for cancellations based on the time and work already invested in the project.

  • Creator Cancellation: If the Creator cancels the project for any reason, a full refund, including the deposit, will be issued.

  • Fees: PayPal processing fees are always covered by the Client and will not be included in any refund amount.
  • No Refunds will be issued once the final product files have been delivered and approved.
